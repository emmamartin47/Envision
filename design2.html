Design 2

This design is a striking fusion of high-fashion and streetwear, seamlessly blending structured tailoring with urban edge. The monochromatic palette of crisp whites and deep grays creates a sophisticated yet modern aesthetic.

Crafted from premium technical fabrics, the ensemble features layered textures and bold silhouettes. The oversized coat, cinched at the waist with a sculptural fabric belt, adds dimension, while metallic accessories and shimmering high-top sneakers inject a futuristic vibe.

Designed for the runway, this look is a statement of contemporary elegance, redefining the boundaries between luxury fashion and street-style innovation.
