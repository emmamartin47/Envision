Design 3

This design is a refined take on modern high-fashion streetwear, seamlessly merging fluid movement with structured elegance. The monochromatic gray palette creates a sophisticated yet effortless aesthetic, embodying contemporary minimalism.

Crafted from premium lightweight fabrics, the ensemble features a flowing, belted wrap coat with a dramatic silhouette, paired with sleek shorts for a bold contrast. Metallic high-top sneakers add a futuristic edge, while subtle accessories enhance the overall refined look.

Designed for the runway, this piece redefines urban luxury, balancing power and grace in a statement of cutting-edge fashion.
